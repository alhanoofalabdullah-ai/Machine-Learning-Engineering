# 🚀 Project 18 – Energy Consumption Prediction

Enterprise AI-powered Energy Consumption Prediction Platform for forecasting energy usage, optimizing utility costs, and supporting sustainability initiatives.

---

# Overview

Organizations consume significant amounts of electricity, water, and energy resources.

This project predicts future energy consumption using historical energy usage data and environmental variables.

The platform helps organizations:

- Forecast energy demand
- Reduce utility costs
- Improve sustainability
- Support carbon reduction goals
- Optimize facility operations
- Enhance energy efficiency

---

# Business Problem

Energy consumption directly impacts operational costs and environmental sustainability.

Organizations require predictive models to:

- Forecast future energy demand
- Detect abnormal usage patterns
- Improve energy efficiency
- Support sustainability initiatives

Machine Learning enables intelligent energy forecasting and optimization.
