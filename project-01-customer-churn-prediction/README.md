# 🚀 Project 01 – Customer Churn Prediction

Enterprise Machine Learning project for predicting customer churn using classification algorithms and customer behavior analytics.

---

# Overview

Customer churn is one of the most important business challenges.

This project predicts whether a customer is likely to leave the company based on historical customer data.

The solution helps organizations:

- Reduce customer loss
- Improve retention strategies
- Increase revenue
- Identify high-risk customers
- Support business decision making

---

# Business Problem

Customer acquisition costs are significantly higher than customer retention costs.

Organizations need predictive models that identify customers who are likely to leave before churn happens.
