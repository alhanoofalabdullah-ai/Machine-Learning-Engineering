# 🚀 Project 17 – Supply Chain Intelligence Platform

Enterprise AI-powered Supply Chain Intelligence Platform designed to optimize logistics, inventory management, procurement planning, and delivery performance using Machine Learning.

---

# Overview

Modern supply chains generate large volumes of operational data across procurement, warehousing, transportation, and inventory systems.

This platform analyzes supply chain performance and predicts operational outcomes to improve efficiency and reduce costs.

The solution helps organizations:

- Optimize inventory levels
- Improve delivery performance
- Reduce logistics costs
- Enhance warehouse operations
- Improve procurement planning
- Support executive decision-making

---

# Business Problem

Supply chain inefficiencies can lead to:

- Delivery delays
- Excess inventory
- Stock shortages
- Increased transportation costs
- Poor customer satisfaction

Machine Learning enables proactive supply chain optimization through predictive analytics.
