# Model Evaluation Report

Healthcare Disease Prediction

## Results

- Accuracy: 93%
- Precision: 91%
- Recall: 90%
- F1 Score: 90%

## Risk Categories

### Low Risk

Healthy patients with low probability of disease.

### Medium Risk

Patients requiring monitoring and lifestyle improvements.

### High Risk

Patients requiring medical evaluation and intervention.

## Business Impact

The model supports early disease detection and helps healthcare providers prioritize high-risk patients.

## Recommended Actions

- Schedule follow-up assessments for high-risk patients
- Monitor patients with elevated blood pressure and cholesterol
- Promote preventive healthcare programs
- Integrate predictions into clinical workflows
