# Model Evaluation Report

## Project

Credit Risk Assessment

## Results

- Accuracy: 91%
- Precision: 89%
- Recall: 87%
- F1 Score: 88%

## Business Impact

The model supports financial institutions in identifying high-risk applicants and improving loan approval decisions.

## Recommended Business Actions

- Review high-risk applications manually
- Apply stricter approval criteria for high debt-to-income ratio
- Use credit score and income stability as core risk indicators
- Monitor approved high-risk customers more frequently
