# 🚀 Project 09 – Sentiment Analysis Platform

Enterprise Natural Language Processing (NLP) platform for sentiment analysis of customer reviews and social media content.

---

# Overview

Organizations receive thousands of customer reviews, feedback comments, and social media mentions every day.

This project analyzes textual feedback and classifies sentiment into:

- Positive
- Neutral
- Negative

The solution helps organizations:

- Improve customer experience
- Monitor brand reputation
- Detect customer dissatisfaction
- Measure customer satisfaction
- Support business decisions

---

# Business Problem

Manual review analysis is slow and difficult to scale.

Organizations need automated sentiment analysis solutions capable of processing large volumes of text data.

Machine Learning and NLP techniques enable automated classification of customer sentiment.
